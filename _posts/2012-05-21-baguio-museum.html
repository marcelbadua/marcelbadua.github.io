The Baguio Museum is the repository of the artifacts of different ethnolinguistic groups of the Cordilleras namely: Apayao, Mountain Province, Ibaloi, Ifugao, Kalinga, Gaddang and the Tinguian or itneg. Though each Cordillera province now has its own museum and collections, the Baguio Museum has an array of collections of traditional attires, accessories, hats, baskets, musical instruments, woodcarving, jars and ceramics and weapons that dates back to the 1950s.
